How does this work?

In this cipher we use m a positive integer and also 𝒫 = 𝒞 = (ℤ26)m where 𝒦 consists of all permutations of {1, 2, …, m}. We define with our key π

eπ(x1,x2,…,xm) = (xπ(1),xπ(2),…,xπ(m))      dπ(y1,y2,…,ym) = (yπ−1(1),yπ−1(2),…,xπ−1(m))

where π−1 is the inverse permutation of π. In other words, the cipher consists of dividing the text into m letter squares and applying to each square the permutation π between the same letters of the square.
