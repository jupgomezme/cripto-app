Cryptography in Arabia



The Arabs were the first to discover and write cryptographic methods more formally. In fact, in the famous collection of stories, Arabian Nights, multiple anagrams and different secret writing schemes are described. Before that in 855d.C the first cryptography book appeared in Arabia and in 1412 an encyclopedia with 14 volumes was written where the concepts of cryptography were explained, it also contains the substitution and transposition techniques (we will call it permutation cryptosystem later) In addition, an explanation of the method of repeated substitutions of each character in the plain text is given.
