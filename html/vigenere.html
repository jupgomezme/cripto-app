x=(x1, x2,..., xm)        y=(y1, y2,..., ym)        k=(k1, k2,..., km)

Where ek(x) = (x1+k1,x2+k2,…,xm+km) y dk(y) = (y1−k1,y2−k2,…,ym−km) to make it easier to understand, we present the following example.
