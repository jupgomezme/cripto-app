Cryptography in the Kama Sutra



In the Kamasutra written by Vatsiaiana and whose full title is Vātsyāyana kāma sūtra ('Vatsiaiana's aphorisms about sexuality'). The author writes that secret writing is one of the 64 arts that a woman must know and exercise, in the text the encryption procedure is described that later we will know how cryptosystem by substitution how to form random pairs of letters and then substitute the original letters by their respective partner. The importance of the couple understanding and practicing cryptography was based on the fact that it is a practical and effective tool to protect the details of marital relationships from outsiders and that messages reach each other or communicate between lovers without being detected.
