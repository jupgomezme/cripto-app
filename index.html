This is an app in which we will find different types of encryption and decryption, such as Displacement, Substitution, Affine, Vigenere, Hill and others that we will be adding over time, we also have the cryptanalysis of the algorithm Displacement and Vigenere. This has been developed by students of the National University since September 2021, in order to fulfill the objectives proposed for the course "Introduction to cryptography and information theory" seen with Professor Agustin Moreno Cañadas of the National University of Colombia who can be contacted via email.

In this subject we are dedicated to study the set of techniques that allow to modify messages so that they can not be read by those who are not authorized to do so and take them to the computer through developed algorithms, these methods are becoming more complex over the years and technological progress that has been presented so far, this science is as old as writing and is used for purposes such as authenticating the identity of users, protect the secrecy of personal communications and commercial and banking transactions, protect the integrity of electronic fund transfers among others.
